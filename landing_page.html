Easy Medic

Simplifying hospital communication with direct and role-based messaging for healthcare professionals.


Features

Never miss a message

See all the messages sent from your colleagues, (whether diagnosis or prescriptions) immediately on your dashboard.

Direct messaging

Send your prescriptions, referrals, diagnosis or any piece of information you would like to pass directly to the professional on call if you know who it is.

Professional messaging

Have no clue who is on call? Not to worry. Send your message to the professional and every one of them would receive it!


Meet the developer!


Inspiration for the project

During my Industrial training period at the Rivers State University Teaching Hospital, I noticed that the health workers communicated through papers, files and forms. This method had a lot of limitations.

  • Doctors truly have terrible handwriting so it was a pain to read the info sent to us!
  • The secretaries getting the results ready had no medical training, so there were misinterpretations and corrections, leading to tremendous delays.
  • There were issues passing information around, and it led to some clashes that could have been easily avoided.


That's where we come in!

With our easy to use features, communication among health workers has never beeen easier!

  • Easily send messages to any colleague with the direct messaging option (if you know there are on call to receive it)
  • If you don't know who's on call? Just send to everyone at once and the guy would respond immediately
  • Send your referrals, diagnosis, prescriptions and whatever workplace info you would like to pass over!
